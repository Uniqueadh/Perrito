“Unopened Memories”

There are photos. I don't delete them. I don't revisit them either. I just keep it on my phone Like something paused, not gone, not alive. I know if I open them, something will come up. Maybe a version of me l've buried. Maybe a feeling I'm not ready to feel again. So l don't touch them. I just let them sit. Quiet reminders of something I once had, and maybe still haven't fully let go.

Not Meant To Happen

I wasn't supposed to fall in love with you, we were supposed to be just friends. But i let my heart slip past the boundaries i promised to stay within. Now, here we are, strangers to each other with memories too heavy to hold but too beautiful to forget. You're the person i never meant to fall for, youre everything i never wanted to lose. I wish i could go back to before i crossed that line, when i could still speak to you without the pain in my chest. when silence between us was comfortable, not heavy. I ruined everything I wasn't supposed to fall in love with you, but i did.

She love being women

You are music constantly fighting its way into my mind a violent, rebellious, but beautiful melody i try to turn down the volume i try anything to turn down the volume i fight i talk i write i travel but at the end of the day i submerge my head in water and i scream why won't you get out of my head?

I love you the way you are even tho you are not with me.

I love you the way you are even when you doubt yourself,
Even when you feel lost or unsure.
Your laughter, your quirks, your gentle heart.
All the little things that make you, you.
I love you not for perfection,
But for your honesty, your kindness, your light.
You are enough, just as you are.
And I love you, simply, for being you.
